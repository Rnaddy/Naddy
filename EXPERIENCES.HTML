1ST VOLUNTEER ACTIVITY

MALACCA MATRICULATION COLLEGE

A picture shows my friends, who also joined me in volunteering. The volunteer activity was requested by the student welfare department at our college. The purpose of this volunteer activity is that we, as volunteers, need to help students lift their belongings on registration day. It is because they can't get help from their friends or family to prevent the COVID-19 disease from spreading. We were the only ones who could help them because we had already undergone quarantine and done some tests to check whether we had the COVID-19 disease or not for a week.
